Hello! I'm Nicole Rothenay!

I am an E-Communications Specialist and aspiring technorhetorician.

I oversee the top tier social media accounts for Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi and act as a social media consultant for the University's departments and organizations. I also manage the construction and dissemination of external e-communications to alumni, donors, and supporters.
After serving 12 years in the Army Reserve, I received my Bachelor of Arts in English from Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi, graduating Summa Cum Laude. I expect to complete my Master of Arts in English in Fall 2019. I developed an interest in e-communications while working as a freelance writer and social media manager for digital marketing agencies and individual companies around the globe; I expanded on this interest in my studies and by seeking a role as New Media Intern with the Marketing & Communications Department. This position led to my current full-time position as the E-Communications Specialist.
In my spare time, I enjoy crafting, coding, writing, and exploring the world with my husband and daughter.
